how to play!






be present.

Only one person should have their device out during gameplay. All other players should put their devices in a place where they cannot be seen. Be present with one another and create ideas together.






explore together.

Work as a team, parent and child together. Everyone should join in in order to maximize the fun!






be playful.

The sky is the limit. There are no wrong answers. The more obscure the better. Try new things and be silly together.
